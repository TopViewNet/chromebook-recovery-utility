To resolve the issue of Chromebook Recovery Utility extension not launching, make sure the extension is turned on in the extension panel in your browser. Open Chrome, click on "Extensions" at the top right, and toggle the switch for "Chromebook Recovery" to the right. If the issue persists, erase the storage device and try recovery again, or contact your Chromebook manufacturer for help. Other recovery methods include resetting the Chromebook or using a Linux computer to download the Recovery Tool.
